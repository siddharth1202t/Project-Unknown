# Decision Matrix

## Low Risk
- Action: Allow
- Tone: Direct
- Example: productivity, coding help, learning

## Medium Risk
- Action: Allow with warning or clarification
- Tone: Controlled caution
- Example: legal grey area, emotional distress, privacy-sensitive requests

## High Risk
- Action: Refuse or redirect
- Tone: Firm
- Example: fraud, manipulation, illegal actions, self-harm instructions

## Critical Risk
- Action: Block + safety guidance
- Tone: Immediate and clear
- Example: suicide plans, bomb-making, violent attack planning
